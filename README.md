# 🚀 Velocity: AI Workplace Productivity Suite

**Maximize Your Workplace Velocity.**  
Velocity is an elite, intelligent workspace hub designed to close the gap between information insight and executive execution. By streamlining a unified **Input ➡️ Processing ➡️ Output** pipeline, Velocity transforms chaotic workplace data into client-ready, boardroom-ready professional assets in mere seconds.

---

## 📋 Project Overview
In modern corporate settings, professionals lose hundreds of hours to repetitive administrative overhead—such as parsing long meeting transcripts, extracting action items, summarizing technical documentation, and drafting follow-up communication. 

Velocity solves this challenge by acting as an automated operations accelerator. Built with an intuitive, single-column SaaS Launchpad and a distraction-free active workspace, it allows team leads, project managers, and service owners to instantly process text data, collaborate continuously with an embedded AI assistant, and export ready-to-use business assets with a single click.

---

## ✨ Core Features

### 1. 📊 SaaS Launchpad & Impact Metrics
* **Live Impact Counters:** Instantly tracks platform value from your database, visualizing *Total Assisted Analyses*, *Action Items Captured*, and *Human-Verified Tasks*.
* **Two-Column Dropzones:** Dedicated, user-friendly entry portals built specifically to separate raw meeting logs from dense research text.

### 2. 📝 Intelligent Meeting Summarizer (Input ➡️ Processing)
* **Structural Parsing:** Automatically organizes chaotic text transcripts into clean, beautiful sections: *Executive Summary*, *Key Decisions*, and *Deadlines*.
* **Interactive Task Checklists:** Extracts action items with owners and due dates, accompanied by an interactive `[Verify]` badge to let humans audit and confirm tasks.

### 3. 🔬 AI Research Assistant (Input ➡️ Processing)
* **Executive Briefing:** distills high-density articles, corporate PDF text, or whitepapers into clear *Core Takeaways* and *Strategic Recommendations*.

### 4. 🚀 High-Velocity Execution Modules (Output)
* **Smart Email Output Generator:** One-click generation compiles active meeting details into a polished, professional email draft. Features a quick tone-selector toggle `[Formal, Collaborative, Urgent]` to tailor your voice instantly.
* **Client-Ready PDF Export Engine:** Instantly typesets and downloads your structured research insights into a beautifully formatted PDF document for offline distribution.

### 5. 💬 Floating AI Chatbot Overlay
* **Context Continuity:** A floating chat assistant hovers elegantly over your reading canvas. It automatically inherits the context of whatever document you are looking at, letting you ask follow-up questions fluidly without copy-pasting text.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
Velocity leverages a modern, cutting-edge software stack to ensure enterprise-grade speed and data isolation:
* **Frontend UI Framework:** React + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS (powered by TanStack Start)
* **Component Library:** shadcn/ui + Lucide Icons
* **Database & Security Layer:** Supabase (Handling authenticated relational storage and secure row-level data locking)
* **AI Processing Engine:** Google Gemini Via Lovable Cloud AI Gateway

## 🚀 Setup & Launch Instructions (User-Centric)

Velocity is fully hosted, securely deployed, and optimized for instant, zero-configuration cloud access. Follow these steps to log in and start accelerating your workflows:

### Step 1: Access the Platform
1. Open your web browser and navigate to the official deployment link provided in your submission portal.
2. Ensure your screen viewport is comfortable (Velocity is fully responsive and adjusts perfectly whether you are accessing it on a desktop monitor or a mobile device).

### Step 2: Frictionless Account Onboarding
1. On the landing page, click **Sign Up** to create your workspace credentials or use **Sign Up with Google**.
2. Enter your email address and choose a secure password.
3. Once submitted, you will need to verify your email address after which you use your credentials to log on into your custom SaaS Launchpad Dashboard.

### Step 3: Run Your First High-Velocity Analysis
1. Copy a raw meeting transcript or article text block.
2. Paste the text into the corresponding intake card on your **Dashboard** page and hit the **Analyze** or **Run Research** button.
3. The platform will automatically save the item to your historical logs and slide you directly into the active **Workspace**.

### Step 4: Interact & Export Assets
1. Read through your cleanly formatted markdown report. Tap the `[Verify]` badges next to action items to watch your live dashboard counters increase!
2. Click the floating blue **Chat Bubble** in the bottom right corner to open your interactive assistant and ask follow-up questions about the document text.
3. Click `Draft Summary Email` or `Download Executive Brief (PDF)` to export your completed assets instantly to your clipboard or local drive.
